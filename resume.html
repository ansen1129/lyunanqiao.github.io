Resume

Hello, my name is Lyu Nanqiao. I am a pianist and a Ph.D. in music education. I am originally from China and I currently live in Russia. I have been a piano teacher since 2016-2018, in China Xi’an Conservatory. In my free time, I teach online, so if you want to have an online piano class, please contact me at any time!
